# CoupleQuests — Child Sexual Abuse & Exploitation (CSAE) Safety Standards

**Last Updated:** 2026
**App Name:** CoupleQuests
**Developer:** CoupleQuests

---

# Our Commitment

CoupleQuests is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and secure environment for all users. We maintain **zero tolerance** for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and strictly prohibit any content, behavior, or interactions that involve minors in sexual or exploitative contexts.

These standards apply to:

* User-generated content
* User profiles
* Messages and interactions
* App submissions
* Community features
* External links shared within the platform

---

# Zero‑Tolerance Policy

CoupleQuests strictly prohibits:

* Any sexual content involving minors
* Sexualized conversations involving minors
* Grooming behavior
* Solicitation involving minors
* Sharing or requesting sexually explicit material involving minors
* Roleplay involving minors in sexual contexts
* Attempting to contact minors for inappropriate purposes
* Linking to external CSAE content

Any violation will result in:

* Immediate content removal
* Permanent account suspension
* Reporting to relevant authorities
* Cooperation with law enforcement when legally required

---

# Age Requirements

CoupleQuests is intended for **users aged 18 and older only**.

We do not allow:

* Users under 18
* Accounts created on behalf of minors
* Content involving minors in romantic or sexual contexts

If we discover underage users:

* The account will be removed immediately
* Associated content will be deleted
